## Property Scoring Criteria

Use this rubric to evaluate potential investments:

### Financial Metrics (40 points)

| Metric | Target | Points |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Monthly Cash Flow | >$400/month | 10 |
| Cash-on-Cash Return | >8% | 10 |
| Cap Rate | >6% | 10 |
| Price-to-Rent Ratio | <120 | 10 |

### Location Metrics (30 points)

| Factor | Target | Points |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Crime Rate | Below city average | 10 |
| School Rating | 5+ (GreatSchools) | 5 |
| Proximity to Jobs | <20 min to major employers | 10 |
| Neighborhood Trend | Improving/stable | 5 |

### Property Metrics (20 points)

| Factor | Target | Points |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Construction Quality | Post-1950, solid structure | 5 |
| Major Systems | Updated (5-10 years) | 5 |
| Rehab Needed | Cosmetic only (<$15K) | 5 |
| Parking | Off-street available | 5 |

### Deal Structure (10 points)

| Factor | Target | Points |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Purchase Price | Below market/ARV | 5 |
| Financing Terms | Favorable rate/terms | 5 |

### Scoring Guide

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 90-100 | Make offer immediately |
| 80-89 | Strong consideration |
| 70-79 | Proceed with caution |
| <70 | Pass |

## Due Diligence Checklist

### Before Offer

- [ ] Drive neighborhood at day/night
- [ ] Check crime statistics (SpotCrime, City-Data)
- [ ] Verify rental comps (Rentometer, Zillow, Craigslist)
- [ ] Review recent sales comps
- [ ] Check flood zone status
- [ ] Research city permits required
- [ ] Estimate accurate rehab costs

### Under Contract

- [ ] Professional inspection
- [ ] Title search and review
- [ ] Survey (if needed)
- [ ] Utility bill history review
- [ ] Lease review (if tenant-occupied)
- [ ] Property tax verification
- [ ] HOA docs (if applicable)
- [ ] Insurance quotes
- [ ] Property management interview

### Before Closing

- [ ] Final walkthrough
- [ ] Verify clear title
- [ ] Confirm financing
- [ ] Set up LLC bank account
- [ ] Arrange property insurance
- [ ] Coordinate with property manager
- [ ] Plan renovation timeline
