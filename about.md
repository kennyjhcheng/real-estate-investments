# About Our Investment Group

## Mission

To build a portfolio of cash-flowing rental properties in Michigan that generates passive income and long-term wealth for our partnership.

## Why Michigan?

1. **Affordability**: Entry prices 25-40% below national average
2. **Cash Flow**: Strong rent-to-price ratios
3. **Local Advantage**: Team member with contracting, accounting, and personal connections
4. **Market Timing**: Stabilizing market with modest growth projected
5. **Diversification**: Different economic drivers than our current locations

## Investment Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Cash Flow First**: We prioritize monthly cash flow over speculation on appreciation
2. **Value-Add**: Leverage our contractor advantage to force appreciation
3. **Risk Management**: Conservative underwriting, strong reserves, quality tenants
4. **Operational Excellence**: Professional property management, documented systems
5. **Long-Term Hold**: Build generational wealth through long-term ownership

### What We Won't Do

- ❌ Over-leverage or risky financing
- ❌ Neglect due diligence to "get a deal done"
- ❌ Compromise on tenant screening
- ❌ Skip property inspections
- ❌ Ignore local landlord-tenant laws
